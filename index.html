The notebook

More than a notebook. A tool made to stay.

Lumen Notes combines the solid presence of aluminum with the timeless simplicity of paper. From the outside, it feels like a precise modern object. Inside, there are no batteries, no notifications, and no algorithms. Only pages, ideas, sketches, and the human hand.

It was designed by a designer for creators who still believe that the first line on paper can become the beginning of something real.

Brand story

Where ideas catch light.

The name Lumen comes from the idea of light: clarity, direction, hope, and the beginning of something new. Every idea starts as a small spark before it grows into something tangible.

A sketchbook is often where that first spark appears. It is where thoughts become lines, and lines become projects, products, and stories.

The aluminum cover naturally reflects light, just as a notebook reflects ideas. Lumen Notes was created to become a place where inspiration can be captured, explored, and transformed into something real.

Built to age with you

A notebook that records both ideas and time.

Every scratch, mark, and sign of use becomes part of its story. Like a trusted camera, travel case, lighter, or leather journal, Lumen Notes is designed to gain character through use, not lose value because of it.

For sketching and notes

Made for creators who think visually.

We call it a sketch notebook because creative work rarely separates writing from drawing. A page may hold a sentence, a diagram, a quick section, a vehicle silhouette, a building idea, a garment concept, or a process map.

Lumen Notes is designed for people who think with a pencil in their hand.
